2025-07-23 Extended passports become invalid on 1 October As of 1 October 2025, Swedish authorities will no longer accept passports that have had their period of validity extended by inserting a stamp or sticker into the passport.
If you have such a national passport, you should apply for a new one.
2025-07-07 If you need to apply for a visa, you can now do so in Islamabad As of 7 July, you can apply for a Schengen visa in Pakistan for short visits to Sweden by submitting your application in Islamabad.
The Swedish Migration Agency is responsible for considering applications from people who want to live in Sweden, seek protection or become Swedish citizens.
