The method of assessment will vary but could include: Passing one of the Home Office's secure English language tests (SELTs) at an approved test centre.
Having a GCSE, A Level or Scottish equivalent in English language or literature following education at a UK school whilst below the age of 18.
You can also meet the English language requirement if you have already met the English language requirement successfully in a previous application made either inside or outside the UK.
This requirement can only be satisfied if the level of English in the previous application was at the required level for the current application.
