If your family is accompanying you to Sweden, they can be granted a residence permit for the same period as you, but never longer than the period of validity of their passports.
What does it cost to apply? Employees: SEK 1,500.
Adult family members: SEK 1,500 Children: SEK 750 Citizens of Japan do not pay an application fee.
You will not receive a refund if your application is rejected.
How long must I wait for a decision? If an application for a residence permit for research is complete, we will make a decision within 30 days.
It is not possible to say exactly how long you will need to wait for a decision.
