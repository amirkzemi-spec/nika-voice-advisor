Start at university departments for funding guidance.
They provide insights into paid doctoral positions and scholarships that align closely with your field of study.
Resources Check out the Research Council of Finland and Research.fi for research funding possibilities.
Paid doctoral positions may also be on offer - you can search for them for example via the Euraxess.fi platform, or on the universities' websites .
For further information, contact your Finnish university department.
EDUFI Fellowships - programme discontinued in October 2025 The EDUFI Fellowships programme for Doctoral students and researchers has been discontinued, so applications to that programme are no longer considered.
