Application Options Joint Application: Apply to up to six programmes with one application in January for autumn (September) study start.
Limited programmes are also available in September, for studies starting in January.
Separate Applications: For programs outside the joint application periods, details and application schedules vary, so check the programme search at Studyinfo.fi or connect with the institutions directly.
If you are a non-EU/EEA applicant, you need to pay an application fee .
Eligibility Bachelor’s Programmes: You'll need a high-school diploma that qualifies you for higher education in your home country.
Master’s Programmes: A bachelor’s degree is required.
