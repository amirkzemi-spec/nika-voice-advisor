Photo: Cecilia Larsson Lantz/Imagebank.sweden.se The Swedish Migration Agency has set a new 30-day target for concluding residency permit applications from foreign PhD students and their dependents, in what it hopes will mean better service for higher education.
Advertisement Sweden's government has ordered the agency to shorten processing times and increase the proportion of decisions made within the statutory time frame (in other words, within 30 days), especially when it concerns residency permits for work, study and research.
