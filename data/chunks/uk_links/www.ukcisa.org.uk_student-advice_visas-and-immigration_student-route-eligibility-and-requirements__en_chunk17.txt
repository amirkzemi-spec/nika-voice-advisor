You cannot apply as a Child Student.
If you want to study an English language course which is below level B2 and lasts between six to 11 months, or if you want to learn English at an institution which does not have a Student sponsor licence, you can normally come to the UK as a Short-term student instead.
Re-sits and repeats Your institution may be able to to issue a CAS so you can apply as a Student to re-sit an exam or to repeat a module as part of your current course.
You will be granted enough permission to re-sit the exam or repeat the module.
