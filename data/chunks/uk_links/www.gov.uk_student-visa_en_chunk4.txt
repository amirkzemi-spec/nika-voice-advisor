Staying longer in the UK You may be able to: extend your visa if you’re eligible, for example to continue your studies in the UK switch to a Student visa from another visa if you’re already in the UK switch to a Graduate visa A Graduate visa will allow you to stay in the UK after successfully completing your course for: 2 years if you apply on or before 31 December 2026 18 months if you apply on or after 1 January 2027 It will allow you to stay for 3 years if you completed a PhD or other doctoral qualification.
When you can travel to the UK You can arrive before your course starts.
