If you have already paid all of your course fees, or for study abroad or for another course with no fees, your CAS will confirm this.
Living costs The Home Office uses fixed amounts, which may or may not reflect your actual living costs.
If you wish to study in London, you will need £1,529 for each month of your course, up to a maximum of nine months.
This means that if you will be studying in London for a course that lasts one month only, the amount that you will need is £1,529.
If you will be studying in London for a course lasting nine months or more, the amount that you will need is £13,761.
If you are not sure whether you will be studying in London, ask your Student sponsor.
