If you are relying on using a joint bank account in which you are one of the account holders, please note that your name must be on the account along with the other named individual.
You can use more than one account if necessary, and you can use a combination of your account(s) and your parent(s) or legal guardian(s) account(s).
You can use an overseas bank account.
If your funds are not in British pounds (GBP), you should convert the closing balance into GBP using OANDA and the conversion rate on the date on which you make your application.
