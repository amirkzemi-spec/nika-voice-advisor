Apply outside the UK As part of your application, you’ll need to prove your identity.
How you do this depends on where you’re from and what type of passport you have.
You’ll either: use the ‘UK Immigration: ID Check’ app to scan your identity document - you’ll also create or sign into your UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI) account have your fingerprints and photo (biometric information) taken at a visa application centre You’ll be told what you need to do when you apply.
