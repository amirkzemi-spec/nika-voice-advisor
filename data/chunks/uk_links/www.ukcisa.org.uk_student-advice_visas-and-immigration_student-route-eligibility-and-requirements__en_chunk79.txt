Below degree-level courses If you are under 18, the time limits are not applicable to your Student route application.
From your 18th birthday, you can spend up to two years on a Student visa or with Student permission studying courses that lead to a qualification below degree level .
You can spend the two years studying a single course or several courses.
The time limit applies, whether you apply under the Student route inside or outside the UK.
Time you have spent studying with any other type of immigration permission is not counted.
