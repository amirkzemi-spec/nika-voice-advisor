If you are applying from outside the UK for entry clearance or if you have been living in the UK with immigration permission for less than 12 months, you must meet the following financial requirements: Course fees You must have enough money to pay for the course fees for the first year of your course, or the entire course if it is less than one year.
If your CAS has been issued for further study on a course that you are already part-way through, the 'first year of study' means the first year of this new period of study.
The Home Office will use the details in your CAS to confirm how much money you need for your course fees.
