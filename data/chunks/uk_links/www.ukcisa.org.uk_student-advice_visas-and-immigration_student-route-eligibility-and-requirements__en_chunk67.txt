Academic Technology Approval Scheme (ATAS) An Academic Technology Approval Scheme (ATAS) certificate is issued by the Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office (FCDO), which gives you security clearance to study certain subject areas where the knowledge gained may have application in the development of weapons of mass destruction (for example, certain science subjects, mathematics, engineering, technology or medicine).
If your course leads to a qualification at master's or doctorate level, a postgraduate qualification abroad, or you are a Short-term student , ask the university whether you need an ATAS clearance certificate for the course.
