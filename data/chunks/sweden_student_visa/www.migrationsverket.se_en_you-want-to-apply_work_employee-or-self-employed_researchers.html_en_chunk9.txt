Consent that the child may move to Sweden If a child has two parents/legal guardians and the child’s other parent/legal guardian will be remaining in their country of origin, you must attach a statement of consent from the other parent/legal guardian to the application.
You must also attach a copy of the other parent or legal guardian’s passport or other identity document.
