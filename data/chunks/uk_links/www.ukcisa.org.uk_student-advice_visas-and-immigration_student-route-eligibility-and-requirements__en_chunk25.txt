If your passport will expire at any other point during your time in the UK, you should check with the passport issuing authority in your home country if you will be able to replace it in the UK.
This is possible for most nationalities, but not all.
English language requirements When you apply for the Student route, you will be assessed on your ability to read, write, speak and understand English.
Your Student sponsor will tell you how it will assess your English and the level of English you must have, which is subject to minimum levels set by the Home Office.
