Letter from your bank, or a regulated financial institution As evidence of your money, you can use a letter from your bank or regulated financial institution in your Student route application.
Maintenance must be in the form of cash funds and immediately accessible.
You cannot use evidence of other types of finances, such as shares, bonds, or a pension fund.
You also cannot use an overdraft facility or a credit card.
Funds acquired in the UK must also have been lawfully obtained and cannot have been obtained if you were in breach of your Student visa or Student permission conditions, or in the UK unlawfully.
